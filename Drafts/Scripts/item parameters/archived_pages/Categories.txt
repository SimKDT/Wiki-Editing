==== Categories ====
Parameter for weapons. Specifies the category for the game to correctly recognize the skill for this weapon. Categories: Axe, Spear, SmallBlade, LongBlade, Blunt, SmallBlunt.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
Categories = SmallBlade,
