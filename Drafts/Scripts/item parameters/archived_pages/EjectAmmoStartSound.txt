==== EjectAmmoStartSound ====
Parameter for weapons. This parameter specifies the sound that will be played when the weapon starts to unload.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
EjectAmmoStartSound = DoubleBarrelShotgunEjectAmmoStart,
</syntaxhighlight>
