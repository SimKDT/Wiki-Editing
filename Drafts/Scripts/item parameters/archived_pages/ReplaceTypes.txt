==== ReplaceTypes ====
Used to specify an item to replace when the item is filled with something. Written in the format "Source ResultItem; Source2 ResultItem2".
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
ReplaceTypes = PetrolSource WaterBottlePetrol;WaterSource WaterBottleFull,
</syntaxhighlight>
