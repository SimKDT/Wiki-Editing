==== RemoveNegativeEffectOnCooked ====
If this parameter is true, then the item will remove the negative effects of thirst, boredom and unhappiness after cooking.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
RemoveNegativeEffectOnCooked = true,
</syntaxhighlight>
