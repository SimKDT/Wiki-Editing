==== KnockBackOnNoDeath ====
If the parameter is set to true, then when hit with this weapon, the player will receive a small amount of experience in the strength skill.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
KnockBackOnNoDeath = true,
</syntaxhighlight>
