==== AttachmentsProvided ====
Defines the hotbar slots added when the item is equipped, and therefore the available [[AttachmentType|attachments]] and attached location on the character for the item occupying the slot. These are defined in <code>ISHotbarAttachDefinition.lua</code>.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
AttachmentsProvided = HolsterLeft;HolsterRight,
</syntaxhighlight>
