==== AttachmentType ====
Defines the attachment type, and therefore the [[AttachmentsProvided|hotbar slot]] the item can be added to, along with the AttachedLocation group, as defined in <code>AttachedLocations.lua</code>. These attachment types are defined as <code>attachments</code> in <code>ISHotbarAttachDefinition.lua</code>.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
AttachmentType = Rifle,
</syntaxhighlight>
