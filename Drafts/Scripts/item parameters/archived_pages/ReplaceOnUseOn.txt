==== ReplaceOnUseOn ====
Used to specify an item to replace when the item is filled with something. Written in the format "Source" - "ResultItem".
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
ReplaceOnUseOn = WaterSource-WaterPot,
</syntaxhighlight>
