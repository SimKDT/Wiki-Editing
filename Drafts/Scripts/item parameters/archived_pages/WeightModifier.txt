==== WeightModifier ====
Parameter for WeaponPart. Determines how the weapon part affects the weight of the weapon. A positive number adds weight, a negative number decreases it.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
WeightModifier = 0.5,
</syntaxhighlight>
