==== HungerChange ====
Used for food. Indicates how much hunger the food will satisfy if the item is completely eaten. If the number is negative, hunger will decrease, if positive, it will increase.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
HungerChange = -20,
</syntaxhighlight>
