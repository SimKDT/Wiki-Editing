==== SoundParameter ====
A modifier that affects the noise from an item when it is equipped (InventoryContainer). First parameter is EquippedBaggageContainer, second parameter is (Values: None, HikingBag, DuffleBag, PlasticBag, SchoolBag, ToteBag, GarbageBag)
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
SoundParameter = EquippedBaggageContainer HikingBag,
</syntaxhighlight>
