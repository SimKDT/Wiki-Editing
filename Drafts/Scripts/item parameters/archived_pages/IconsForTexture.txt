==== IconsForTexture ====
The names of the icons are specified, which in order correspond to the possible textures of the item (as specified in the .xml file of the clothing).
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
IconsForTexture = BandanaBlack;BandanaBlue;BandanaRed,
</syntaxhighlight>
