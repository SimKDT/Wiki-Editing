==== ThirstChange ====
Used for food. Indicates how much thirst the food will quench if the item is completely consumed. If the number is negative, thirst will decrease, if positive, it will increase.
<syntaxhighlight lang="Java">
ThirstChange = -4,
</syntaxhighlight>
